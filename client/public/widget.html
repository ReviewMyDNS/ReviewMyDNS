Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is the widget free to use?

Yes, the DNS lookup widget is completely free to embed on any website.

Will the widget slow down my site?

No. The widget is loaded within an iframe asynchronously, meaning it loads independently of the rest of your content and will not impact your page's load performance or Core Web Vitals.
